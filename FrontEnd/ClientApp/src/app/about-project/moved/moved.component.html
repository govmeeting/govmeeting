Govmeeting provides the means to produce verbatim transcripts of public meeting. It then processes those transcripts to extract all pertinent information:
  • Bills that are proposed, debated or voted upon.
  • Issues that were discussed.
  • If meetings transcripts are available, it will use those.
  • If meetings recordings are available, it will generate transcripts using speech recognition.
  • Also., a smartphone app will be available for a volunteer to record a meeting and upload it.

Transcripts made from speech reconition must be proofread. Here is the start of software being developed to proofread a transcript. It splits the work into small size chunks that can be simultaneously worked on by a few community volunteers.
