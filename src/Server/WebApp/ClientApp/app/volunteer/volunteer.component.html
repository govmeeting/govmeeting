Future Volunteer Page

This will be a page where volunteers can collaborate. The default content displayed will that related to their own home community. They can also browse content related to other communities. The page will include these elements:

  • List of current and past meetings and their status.

    This will show a listing of present and past meetings. It will show which have been processed and which still require work. If the user selects a meeting, either the "Work in Progres" or "Completed" details components below will be shown.

  • Work in Progress

    This will show the current state of progress on this meeting. If this meeting involves a recording, it will display a list of all the pieces that the recording was split into. It will show which pieces are (1) completed, (2) in progress and (3) not started.
    The volunteer can choose a piece of the recording to work on. Once someone start to process a piece, it is assigned to them until they either mark it completed or some (to be determined) time span elapses.
    The volunteer can also choose to work on adding tags to a transcript that is ready for this step. In this case, the transcript will be assigned to them, until it is completed or after some time lapse.

  • Completed

    This will show statistics on work that was completed: Number of volunteer contributing, time to complete, etc.

  • Volunteer chat box

    Volunteer who are logged in can chat among themselves.
